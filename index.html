ABOUT KIREINA

Kireina Industry Co., Ltd.

Kireina Industry Co., Ltd. is one of the earliest manufacturers engaged in researching, developing, producing and selling smart film and smart glass. We commit ourselves to the field of global construction materials, home automation ,windows, doors and glass, automotive and supporting theses fields with high quality smart films and smart glass. Kireina films consist of imported high permeability liquid crystal film and high quality automotive float glass. Our products have been guaranteed quality management ISO9001 and environmental management system ISO14001. Transmittance is up to 82% （average is 77% , domestic）, hazy degree is less than 3.4%, (average is 4.5%,domestic). The power rating is less than 4W/sqm. All the parameters are superior than international level.
