Benefits of Nano-Ceramic Technology in Films

Window films can only provide the type of protection they’re engineered to provide, meaning subpar films create subpar results. Small ceramic materials with spectrally selective technology creates a reliable shield, catching a large amount of the rays and heat that would normally go right through windows.

  • Superb durability – resistance to salt-content environment.
  • UV rejection 100 percent dye-free & 100% metal-free.
  • Natural tone with low reflectivity.
  • High total solar energy rejection.
  • High ultra-violet rejection >99%.
  • Scratch resistant hardcoat.

Benefits:

  • Increases comfort cooler interior.
  • Protects against sun-related skin problems.
  • Reduces glare and fading to interior upholstery.
  • Enhance overall aesthetics with a soft natural shade.
  • Saves energy by reducing fuel/electricity consumption.
  • 5 year product warranty – no demetallization and discoloration.
  • Minimizes interferences with GPS systems and cellular phone.
  • Increases safety with improved glass shatter-resistance.
