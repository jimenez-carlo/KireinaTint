Factory Introduction


Shanghai HOHO Industry was established in 2005 Located at Shanghai China, Near Pudong International Airport and Hongqiao Airport ,Which is a manufacture that own registered brand like HOHO ,SUNICE etc... we dedicated our passion to globalize our products and business development . The company possesses clean workshop 20,000sqmeter and more than 2000 sqmeter Warehousing and logistics center, and with a professional installation team.

HOHO mainly manufactures smart film, smart glass, auto window tint film, building film etc. Which are mainly applied in automobile decorating and home building fields . for smart film, annual production up to 60000 square meters, Our products exported more than 200 countries countries because of the brilliant quality ,competitive price and excellent after sale service.

HOHO is aiming at becoming the first choice brand in smart film and smart glass. The company has a construction team of dozens of installers since its establishment. Our members have rich experience in partition design, smart glass and film installations. The team provides customers with a coordinated service from measurement to design and installation to after-sale service. HOHO have finished more than 5000 projects in film and glass installations in hotels, airports, schools, hospitals, shopping malls, conferences rooms etc.


“HOHO Brand, Global Service； HOHO Quality, Trouble-free Lifetime”

In addition, We invented a new series of ultra-insulated, anti-UV nano ceramic skin-care solar film. 100% UV rejection and 99% IR rejection which reshaped the industry quality standards of Automotive film in China. From manufacturing to marketing management, HOHO has achieved refined operation , is and will be always committed to enabling clients to enjoy better quality. During more than 10 years of development, we actively explored the market and gradually developed into a global enterprise with business in more than 200 countries and regions around the world.

Finally We sincerely welcome you to visit our company to discuss long-term business cooperation.
